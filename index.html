MiG-29 Fighter Jet and Missiles

About the MiG-29

The MiG-29 is a Russian-made multirole fighter jet known for its versatility and performance in combat. It is capable of carrying a wide range of air-to-air and air-to-ground missiles, making it a formidable aircraft in modern warfare.

Missiles the MiG-29 Can Carry

  • R-73 (AA-11 Archer) - A short-range infrared-guided missile.
  • R-77 (AA-12 Adder) - A medium-range radar-guided missile.
  • Kh-31 - A high-speed anti-ship missile.
  • Kh-25 - An air-to-ground missile for precision strikes.
